Landing Page

A landing page is perfect for any small business that operates from social media. If you're anything like us, your brand spans across multiple platforms with an intricate web of links connecting everything together. Right? A landing page is a way to showcase all your top social media posts and consolidate your links in one spot.

How it works: Each page is custom designed. Just give us some guidelines on how you want the layout and we'll create a custom page for you. If you already have logos, pictures, etc, just send those in and we will include them with your page.

Let's talk about price: the base price includes one complete webpage in any format you want. If you already purchased a domain and hosting, we can send you the .zip file for you to upload with your hosting provider. Or just sit back and relax and let us worry about the setup. This package includes free hosting and free domain transfers. If you already purchased a domain and don't want to transfer, don't even worry: we can still host and walk you through how to point your DNS to our servers. If you need a domain name, we are happy to purchase one for you (subject to availability). We will just add the price of the domain to you invoice.

Did we mention you can split up your payments? How about over 3 months? No credit cards or automatic billing. We just send you an invoice for each month and if you are unable to pay, we'll just stop service to your page. No late fees, no interest, no weird stipulations and nothing to worry about. Okay so I know what you're thinking: what if I need to update content on my page? Again, we got you covered with the flat fees. We charge a flat $20 service fee for each update. We'll send you a preview of the updates you requested and once payment clears, we'll send it live.
